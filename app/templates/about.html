{% block title %} About The Cat Adoption Agency {% endblock %}

Hey all, I hope you're having fun with this app. As a bit of background, it was very heavily inspired by the mobile game Neko Atsume created by Hit-Point. In fact, I loved Neko Atsume so much that I used the characters directly as motivation. Miss Fortune is my favourite cat, by the way. You can have a look at their website, and I'd also recommend giving it a try if you want a zero effort, comfy mobile game.

My second source of inspiration was Neopets. Not everyone can have a real pet, but anyone can have Saint Purrtrick if no one steals him from you. All these cats have traits and personalities that define them, and until quite recently, it would have been very difficult to create the perfect stories and backgrounds that accurately reflect them. However, with the help of Python and Gemini, not only was the process relatively quick and painless, but the results are probably better than what I could write myself! So I'm quite happy and am looking forward to other ways that I can apply generative AI.

If you want to see my other projects, you can check out my Github or my website!

Until next time, happy cat adopting.
